Hamburger

Description

A simple dish, yet such an excellent classic when prepared the right way: with fresh ingredients and a sprinkle of love.

Ingredients

  • Buns, freshly baked if possible
  • An onion
  • Lettuce, arugula or iceberg, depending on taste
  • Tomato
  • Pickles
  • Cheese, Cheddar, American or Gouda
  • Ground pork and beef, ideally 30/70 mix but 50/50 is fine
  • Mixture of spices
  • Mayo, Ketchup, Mustard and pickle juice

Steps

  1. Slice and dice the vegetables according to your liking.
  2. Mix the ground pork and beef with the mixture of spices and form balls the size of around 70-100 grams (they should fit in your hand).
  3. Butter the pan on medium to high heat, as soon as the pan is hot put 2 to 3 balls in the pan and smash them with the help of a spatula.
  4. As soon as they start to brown on one side turn them over and top them off with 1 or 2 slices of your cheese of choice and let them get some color on the other side.
  5. Whilst the burgers are doing their thing, grab a small cup and mix some equal parts of mayo, ketchup, mustard and a little bit of pickle juice. This will be the sauce for our burgers.
  6. When the burgers are done butter the buns and put them on the pan on medium to high heat for 30-45 seconds.
  7. Finally, assemble the perfect burger! Sauce up both buns with some sauce, put on the lettuce, onion, tomato, pickles and the burger(s) with cheese and voil√°!
