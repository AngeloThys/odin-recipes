Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

A meal I would often looked forward to after a long day of school and after school activities; one of the many dishes my mother used to prepare just for me.

Ingredients

  • Pasta, in this case, spaghetti
  • 500 gr. of ground beef
  • An onion
  • 2-3 carrots
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Finely dice the onion and carrots
  2. Olive oil the pan and put it on medium heat
  3. Toss the diced vegetables in the pan and let them simmer for a while
  4. Salt and pepper the ground beef
  5. As soon as the vegetables are softened up a bit insert the ground beef in the pan with the vegetables
  6. Remember to break up the ground beef as it sears so it doesn't clump together
  7. When the ground beef has turned from pink to grey it's time to add the tomato sauce and lower the heat
  8. Stir and let it sit for 2-3 minutes and you're all done!
